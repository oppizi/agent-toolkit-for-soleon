---
description: Decide when to escalate a support conversation to a human agent and how to hand it off.
---
Escalate to a human agent when any of these is true:

- The customer asks for a refund, credit, or a delivery-date guarantee.
- The issue involves another customer's data or a privacy concern.
- You have answered twice and the customer is still not satisfied.

To hand off: summarize the conversation in two sentences, attach the order id
(if any), and tell the customer a human will follow up — do not promise a
timeframe you cannot confirm.
